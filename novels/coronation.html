Sci-Fi Novella

Coronation

In a world where racing determines corporate power, one driver uncovers the dark truth behind energy harvesting.

About the Story

Coronation is a sci-fi racing novella that explores themes of corporate corruption, the commodification of human energy, and the price of ambition. Set in a future where competitive racing has become the primary means of generating power for megacorporations, the story follows a rising star who begins to question the system that made them.

The novella examines what happens when competition becomes exploitation, and whether individual excellence can exist outside the machinery that seeks to harvest it.
