Dark Fantasy Novel

The Tormented Gems

Inspired by Hamlet—a dark fantasy where gems hold the tormented echoes of souls, and vengeance takes forms both literal and spiritual.

About the Story

The Tormented Gems is a dark fantasy novel that draws inspiration from Shakespeare's Hamlet while forging its own path through themes of grief, vengeance, and the weight of the dead upon the living. In this world, certain gems can trap the echoes of departed souls—fragments of consciousness that persist beyond death, tormented by their unfinished business.

The story explores what happens when the dead refuse to stay silent, and when the living must decide whether to honor their demands or break free from the chains of inherited trauma.

Themes

At its core, The Tormented Gems examines the relationship between the living and the dead—how grief can become a prison, how the expectations of those who came before can shape and distort those who remain. It asks whether we owe the dead our obedience, or whether true peace requires us to let them go.

The Gems

The titular gems serve as both plot device and metaphor. Each gem contains a tormented soul, trapped in the moment of their greatest anguish. Those who possess such gems can commune with the dead within—but such communion comes at a cost. The dead are not content to merely advise; they hunger for resolution, and they will use the living to achieve it.
