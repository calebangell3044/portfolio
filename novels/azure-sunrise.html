Fantasy Novella

The Azure Sunrise

A meditation on transformation and preservation, following two souls bound by fate and philosophy.

About the Story

The Azure Sunrise follows Solen and Epheira on a journey that questions the very nature of change. In a world where the Azure God's influence spreads like dawn across the horizon, two individuals must confront what it means to transform—and what is lost when we do.

This novella explores the tension between preservation and transformation that lies at the heart of the interconnected universe. It asks whether change is salvation or destruction, and whether the answer depends on who's asking.

Characters

Solen — A seeker caught between worlds, whose journey toward the Azure God forces them to question everything they believed about identity.

Epheira — Bound to Solen by circumstances beyond their control, Epheira represents the tension between duty and desire, preservation and release.
