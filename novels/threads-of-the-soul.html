Threads of the Soul

The Orphan's War — Where souls are bound by Ideals, and war is waged with philosophy as much as steel.

About the Story

Threads of the Soul: The Orphan's War is the first book in a planned trilogy that forms the cornerstone of the interconnected universe. In a world where magic flows through the soul and is shaped by philosophical Ideals, five protagonists find their fates intertwined by forces beyond their understanding.

The magic system is built on the tension between two fundamental Ideals: Transformation—the belief that change is the path to perfection—and Preservation—the conviction that what exists must be protected and maintained. Those who bind themselves to an Ideal gain power, but at the cost of being shaped by it.

The Binding Ideals

At its heart, this is a story about the nature of belief and how the philosophies we embrace come to define us. The Binding Ideals aren't just a magic system—they're a lens through which to examine how ideology shapes identity, and whether we control our beliefs or they control us.

Point of View Characters

The story follows five POV characters, each representing different facets of the conflict between Transformation and Preservation. Their arcs interweave across a war that threatens to reshape the world itself.

Status
