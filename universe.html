Worldbuilding

The Interconnected Universe

A cosmological framework connecting multiple stories through shared magic systems, gods, and the fundamental tension between Transformation and Preservation.

The Vision

Like Brandon Sanderson's Cosmere, this interconnected universe is designed to be a tapestry of stories that can be enjoyed independently but reward readers who explore multiple works. Each novel and novella shares fundamental cosmological rules while telling its own distinct story.

The connecting tissue isn't just Easter eggs or cameos—it's a shared understanding of how magic, souls, and the divine interact across all worlds and stories.

The Binding Ideals

At the foundation of all magic lies the conflict between two philosophical forces: Transformation and Preservation. These aren't merely abstract concepts—they are fundamental aspects of reality itself, and those who align themselves with one Ideal or the other gain access to power that reflects their chosen philosophy.

Transformation holds that change is the path to perfection. Nothing should remain static; growth, evolution, and metamorphosis are sacred. Those bound to Transformation can reshape reality—but they themselves are changed by every act of power.

Preservation holds that what exists has value precisely because it exists. To protect, maintain, and defend is sacred. Those bound to Preservation can shield, restore, and anchor—but they must accept that their power resists the natural flow of change.

The Pantheon

The gods of this universe are not creators but embodiments—manifestations of different aspects of the Binding Ideals. Each god represents a particular expression of Transformation or Preservation, and their interactions with mortals shape the magic systems of individual worlds.

The Azure God, featured in The Azure Sunrise, represents one such divine expression—a being whose influence spreads like dawn, transforming everything it touches. Other gods embody different facets: preservation through memory, transformation through destruction, preservation through stasis.

Reading Order

Each story is designed to stand alone. However, for those who want to experience the universe in a particular order:

For the core experience: Start with Threads of the Soul, which establishes the fundamental rules. Then explore The Azure Sunrise for a more intimate look at divine power, and The Tormented Gems for the darker implications of the soul mechanics.

For a lighter entry: Coronation can be read completely independently and offers a taste of the thematic concerns without requiring knowledge of the broader cosmology.

Ongoing Development

This universe is actively expanding. New connections between existing works are being discovered, and future stories will continue to build on the established framework while exploring new corners of the cosmology.
